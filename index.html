Frequently asked

What is a credit?

One unit of AI work. A text action (description, title, SEO, translation, extraction) = 1 credit. An image — generated or enhanced — = 50 credits. It’s one shared pool; spend it any way you like.

Do I need an OpenAI or Gemini key?

No. We run and manage the models for you (Claude, Gemini, OpenAI) and route each task to the best one for quality and cost. Prefer your own key? Switch to BYOK on Growth and Scale for unlimited use.

Is this billed per vendor?

No — it’s one subscription per marketplace, managed by the site admin. Your vendors use the AI tools; you own the plan and the credit pool, and can cap per-vendor usage.

I have a huge catalog. Will the credits last?

Monthly credits are sized for ongoing work — new and edited products. For a one-time enrichment of a large existing catalog, switch to BYOK and use your own key with no limit.

Can I switch between monthly and annual?

Yes. Annual saves about 25% over monthly. You can upgrade, downgrade, or switch billing any time.
