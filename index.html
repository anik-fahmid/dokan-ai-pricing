Frequently asked

What is a credit?

One unit of AI work. A text action (description, title, SEO, translation, extraction) = 1 credit. An image — generated or enhanced — = 50 credits. One shared pool; spend it any way.

Do I need an OpenAI or Gemini key?

No. We run and manage the models for you (Claude, Gemini, OpenAI) and route each task to the best one for quality and cost. Prefer your own key? Pro includes BYOK for unlimited use.

Can I give AI to my vendors?

Yes. On Pro you can bundle AI tools into a vendor subscription pack (vendors buy the pack → AI unlocks), or grant AI to specific vendors with per-vendor credit caps.

Is this billed per vendor?

No — one subscription per marketplace, managed by the site admin. You own the plan and the credit pool.

Is there a free option?

Dokan's core AI Assistant is free with your own API key. The plans here are the managed, premium tiers (marketplace builder, semantic search, image gen, reselling).
